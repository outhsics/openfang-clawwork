# Executive Summary

## Objective

Build a standardized reporting package that allows management to compare branch performance, regional rollups, and profitability trends using one consistent monthly structure.

## Key findings

1. West Region branches show the strongest margin performance, led by Branch Delta at `21.6%` operating margin.
2. Branch Beta has the highest revenue volume but only mid-tier profitability because operating expense growth outpaced contribution growth.
3. Branch Gamma is the main underperformer and should be reviewed first for cost discipline and pricing leakage.
4. A repeatable five-tab workbook design is feasible and should reduce manual reporting time in future periods.

## Proposed workbook tabs

1. `Executive Summary`
   Branch rank, regional summary, monthly highlights, and exceptions.

2. `Branch P&L`
   Revenue, direct cost, operating expense, EBITDA, and margin by branch.

3. `Regional Comparison`
   Aggregated branch metrics by region with rank order and variance columns.

4. `Trend View`
   Monthly and yearly comparisons for revenue, EBITDA, and operating margin.

5. `Definitions & Assumptions`
   KPI definitions, refresh notes, and calculation assumptions.

## Management recommendation

Adopt the five-tab package as the standard reporting shell, then automate monthly refreshes around three recurring questions:

- which branches are growing profitably
- which regions are improving or deteriorating
- which operating costs are driving margin compression
