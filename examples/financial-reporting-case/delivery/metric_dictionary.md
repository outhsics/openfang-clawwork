# Metric Dictionary

- `revenue_usd`: gross recognized revenue for the reporting period
- `operating_expense_usd`: branch-level operating expense excluding financing costs
- `ebitda_usd`: earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization
- `operating_margin_pct`: `ebitda_usd / revenue_usd`
- `rank`: descending order by operating margin, used for management comparison
